About Us

Family business since 2001

Uncle Jim, opened the first Satoshi Restaurant in Upper Hill, Nairobi on September 27, 2001. Today, there are 37 areas all through Nairobi, Kisumu, Nakuru, Kericho, Eldoret, Kisii, Kakamega, and Mombasa.

Satoshi Restaurants are well known with a substantial gathering of people including families, kids, seniors, and business experts. Our benevolent condition is perfect for praising unique events, facilitating a business lunch, or assembling for a flavorful dinner with loved ones.
